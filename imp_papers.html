A starting reading list — foundational results and recent landmarks in nanohertz GW astrophysics. Not exhaustive; a springboard.

ApJ 234, 1100 · doi:10.1086/157593
Upper limits on the isotropic gravitational radiation background from pulsar timing analysis
Hellings, R. W. & Downs, G. S. (1983)
ApJ 265, L39 · doi:10.1086/183954
A practical theorem on gravitational wave backgrounds
Phinney, E. S. (2001)
arXiv:astro-ph/0108028
Detection methods for stochastic gravitational-wave backgrounds: a unified treatment
Romano, J. D. & Cornish, N. J. (2017)
Living Rev. Relativ. 20, 2 · arXiv:1608.06889
